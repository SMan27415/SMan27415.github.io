System Analysis and Designing

Most, if not all businesses today function on some sort of computer system for data management, automation of processes, communication, etc. Every business has different requirements that must be met with a computer system. That's why system designing specifically for a business is crucial for success.

In my case, I have built a total of three desktop computers all for personal use.

  1. For mid-high range gaming in 2019.
  2. Helped a friend pick out parts and build his computer for gaming in 2022.
  3. My extremely high-end gaming computer in 2023.

Even though I've built three personal computers, I'm highly knowledgable with system designing for businesses, with a High Distinction in System Designing at my university.

For a business to have a well designed computer system, it must go through the design process:

  1. Analyse the Requirements
  2. Feasibility Study
  3. System Designing
  4. System Development
  5. Integration and Testing
  6. Implementation
  7. Maintenance and Support

This is to ensure the whole development process goes smoothly and the end result is exactly what the client requires.
